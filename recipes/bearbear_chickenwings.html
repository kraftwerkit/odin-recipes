Bearbear's Chicken Wings

Description

He loves when I make simple, wholesome chicken wings!

Ingredients

  • 100 chicken wings
  • 20 minced garlic gloves
  • 1/2 gallon olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. PREPARATION: Toss the chicken wings in a bowl with olive oil.
  2. SEASON: Add the garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. BAKE: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F and bake for an hour
  4. FEED: Gently offer the wings to bear.
